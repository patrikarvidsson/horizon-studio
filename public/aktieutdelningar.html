Projects / Aktieutdelningar

Stock dividends tracker

Aktieutdelningar is an application intended to help users keep track of their annual stock dividends.

It was originally developed by Dick Törnfeldt but was shut down at at the end of 2019 due to lack of time from the creator.

It is currently being rebuilt from the ground up. The goal is for it to better support scalability and to add a myriad of features and UX improvements regularly requested by its users.

Exact ETA is unknown, but it is scheduled to be released at some point in late 2020. Before that, there will be a beta period.
